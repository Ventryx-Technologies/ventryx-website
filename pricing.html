FAQ

Questions teams ask before getting started

Can we upgrade later?

Yes. Teams can start with Free or Starter and move into Growth or Enterprise as automation, workflows, and operational complexity increase.

Who is Growth best for?

Growth is ideal for businesses that need stronger automation, better visibility, controlled approvals, and a scalable operational layer.

Is Enterprise pricing custom?

Yes. Enterprise pricing is tailored around user volume, workflow complexity, integration requirements, onboarding, and governance needs.

Is Operra only for approvals?

No. Operra is built to support workflows, approvals, automation, analytics, access control, and broader internal systems management.
