Platform — Infrastructure

Cloud Services

Ventryx runs on a multi-region cloud infrastructure designed for high availability, low latency, and enterprise-grade reliability — without requiring you to manage any of it.

Global regions

The Ventryx platform is deployed across multiple cloud regions. Traffic is routed to the closest healthy region automatically.

RegionCodeStatus
US East (Virginia)us-east-1Active — primary
US West (Oregon)us-west-2Active

Enterprise plans can pin their data to specific regions for data residency compliance. Contact sales to configure region pinning.

Compute and scaling

All API traffic is served from auto-scaling compute clusters. There are no cold starts, no concurrency limits imposed on your integration, and no capacity to provision. The platform scales transparently to match your request volume.

Storage

Event logs, workflow state, and analytics data are stored in replicated, durable storage with point-in-time recovery. Data is retained according to your plan:
