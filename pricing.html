FAQ

Questions teams usually ask before getting started

Can we upgrade later?

Yes. Teams can start with Free, Starter, or Growth and move into higher tiers as operational needs expand.

Do you offer demos?

Yes. Operra is designed for consultative onboarding, so demo requests are encouraged before rollout.

Is Enterprise pricing custom?

Yes. Enterprise plans are tailored around users, workflow complexity, integrations, and infrastructure requirements.

Can Operra support internal processes beyond approvals?

Yes. Operra is built as an operational platform for workflows, automation, analytics, approvals, and internal systems management.
